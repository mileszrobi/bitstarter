Have you ever noticed that certain types of food were meant to be shared? So have we! Have you ever looked at the pizza on the nearest table wishing you have ordered the same? SO HAVE WE!! Just think about it: pizzas do come in convenient slices. Poultry have conveniently bite sized limbs. Coincidence? We think not! So why settle to one particular flavour? Why not share the food and the experience with others? This is why we want to create The Meal Deal (henceforth: TMD), an incredibly practical app that will gather the foodstuff requirements of your family, workplace, sect, party, congregation, court, or any kind of social gathering.

'This truly is a remarkable development, which I have not foreseen. I wish to start using this fascinating piece of engineering immediately! But how does it work?', we hear you asking (barely suppressing the voices in our heads). It is incredibly easy! You create an event with the food items of your choice (indicating the smallest unit). Your friends then select the number of pizza slices, chicken wings, pints of soda they wish to consume. TMD will then aggregate the needs of the whole assembly and provide you with the details of what you should order. Never again will you have to face the unbearable stress of making the decision between a Margherita and a Pepperoni pizza, or to contemplate what to do with leftovers of your 50 piece bucket of chicken! Share them all.

So what is there to do? What can you expect from a full version? Well, there are a lot of things we can shoot for:
  • The minimum version will contain the option to create events, add the shareable items to them, share the link for the event so that others can vote. Then, TMD will sum up the votes.
  • There could be different ways the summary can be made. Do you want to satisfy everyone? It might mean that you have to order a whole pizza just because someone ordered one slice. Maybe you could optimize to volume? This way everyone would get something to eat, but not everyone would get what they wanted. Ultimately TMD could let you modify the summary, while showing how many people would not be satisfied.
  • You could have your own TMD profile - you could save your event details, food types, etc., so you don't have to set them up each time you use TMD.
  • Of course one of the best things would be to integrate TMD with your favourite restaurants so the created list of meals would directly be sent to them!
  • Whatever you wish - let us know how you think this idea could be enhanced.
