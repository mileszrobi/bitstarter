Have you ever noticed that certain types of food were meant to be shared? So have we! Have you ever looked at the pizza on the nearest table wishing you have ordered the same? SO HAVE WE!! Have you ever ended up ordering a second pizza, which you've devoured in mere seconds in a fiendish delight, then, driven by guilty conscience and a rebelling stomach you've rushed into the loo of the establishment where you've collapsed in tears of self-disgust? Well, we haven't gotten that far yet. But: pizzas do come in convenient slices. Poultry have conveniently bite sized limbs. Coincidence? We think not! So we created The Meal Deal (TMD), an incredibly practical app that will gather the foodstuff requirements of your family, workplace, sect, party, congregation, court, or any kind of social gathering.

'This truly is a remarkable development, which I have not foreseen. I wish to start using this fascinating piece of engineering immediately! But how does it work?', we hear you asking (barely suppressing the voices in our heads). It is incredibly easy! You create an event with the food items of your choice (indicating the smallest unit). Your friends then select the number of pizza slices, chicken wings, pints of soda they wish to consume. TMD will then aggregate the needs of the whole assembly and provide you with the details of what you should order. Never again will you have to face the unbearable stress of making the decision between a Margherita and a Pepperoni pizza, or to contemplate what to do with leftovers of your 50 piece bucket of chicken!
